Protocolo del servidor

/usuarios/nuevo (post)
{user: "esteban", pass: "123456"}
-true: si se guarda
-false: si ya existe user

/usuarios/login (post)
{user: "esteban", pass: "123456"}
-true: Datos correctos
-False: User o pass incorrectos

{num: "1", valor: "1"}  (led 1, valor 1)
